Personalized Dentistry

Dr. Justin Cochell and his team strive to create a personal experience for every patient. Whether you are coming in for a cosmetic procedure such as teeth whitening or a necessary procedure such as a crown, you will leave knowing everything you must about the status of your dental health and what you can do to improve it. Dr. Cochell listens intently to each patient's concerns in order to administer the right treatment for them. As a result, each patient's issue is treated uniquely.

By providing the necessary education to our patients, we feel we are able to establish life-long relationships and provide happy, healthy smiles. Dr. Cochell believes that dentistry should be a pain free, comfortable, and yes, even enjoyable experience! If you are anxious about dental treatment, talk with Dr. Cochell about some of the dental anxiety reduction options that we use. We want your experience at the dentist to be as comfortable as possible.

On our Dental Services page, you can find information about the many cosmetic and general dental procedures that we offer. Once you visit us, Dr. Cochell will evaluate your condition and further explain the procedures that will best benefit you.

Located conveniently three blocks north of Highway 14, across Washougal River Road from Downtown Washougal, we are easily accessible to residents of East Vancouver, Camas, Washougal, and beyond into the Columbia River Gorge.

We are looking forward to meeting you and your family!
